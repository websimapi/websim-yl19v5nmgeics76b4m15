Guide to V0.1 [Click to Open]

Welcome to the Squid Game Simulator!

Save/Load Game: Use the "Save Game State" button below the title to download your entire session.

Starting a Game: Enter the number of players (default 456) and click "Begin new game".

Playing a Round: Use the "Game Presets" box to choose a game, or use the "Custom Round" controls.

Animated Rounds: Click "Arm Animation Mode". Then click any game preset to watch eliminations happen live.

Player Dossier: Click on any player's diamond to open their detailed file.

VIP Betting: In the 'VIP' tab, you can bet on multiple players to win. Your balance persists between games.

Achievements: Unlock achievements by completing certain milestones!
